Who We Are

Twelve Times Modern is a coalition of talented, passionate musicians, who will bring an energizing, dynamic atmosphere to any event. Inspired by classic hits and modern masterpieces, Twelve Times Modern will hit the high note every time, with a familiar yet unique take on your favorite music. We love to play everything - pop, R&B, rock, indie, you name it!
