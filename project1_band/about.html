Our Story

How We Started

Twelve Times Modern was formed just over a decade ago, by a group of friends at the University of Miami who all loved jazz. In a matter of months, Twelve Times Modern was playing at local college bars across Miami, and playing every genre from modern pop to classic rock. Now, over ten years later, Twelves Times Modern is an accomplished, seasoned band that can make your next event a hit.
